    PAVI EXIM SUGAR

    White rice is a processed form of rice in which the husk the outer shell of the seed,the bran the hard outer layer of the grain and the germ the reproductive part of the grain. Due to the removal of the bran, the brown colour associated with whole grain rice is lost. White rice is one of the most widely consumed foods in the world.

    Welcome To Pavi

    Export In India

    Pavi Exim (OPC) Pvt. Ltd. are Exporter Fruits, Vegetables & Other Products from India to Other Countries. We also export Spice products & Grains to other countries. If you are looking for an organization that is obsessed with providing the best of services at incredible delivery speeds, you have come to the right place. Pavi Exim has consistently maintained its quality ever since 2012. We have a proud record of successful exports in the agricultural sector. Our global presence and acceptance as one of the best dealers in fresh agricultural produce sets us apart from our competition.

    Over the years, we have built a strong relationship with both our suppliers and retailers. With our qualified team of experts, we have engineered a way to keep all our products wholesome, hygienic, and as fresh as the day they arrived from the fields. We are Exporter Fruits & Vegetables From India.
