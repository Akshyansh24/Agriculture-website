What We Do ?

One of the leading exporters of farm-fresh agricultural products, Pavi Exim (OPC) Pvt. Ltd, has an impeccable track record over the past few years. We export a wide range of fruits and vegetables, rice, and spices to international locations such as Sri Lanka, Dubai, Qatar, Australia, USA , Canada and Germany. Our service coverage stretches across all areas of the globe. Along with international business, we service areas in India, such as Maharashtra, Tamil Nadu, Madhya Pradesh and Gujrat to name a few. Our prime exports include fresh produce such as juicy red tomatoes, the sweetest strawberries, long and fragrant basmati rice, hot and spicy chillies and ripe, glistening pomegranates. You can rest assured that your produce reaches you in perfect condition. We take quality control very seriously here. All our products are packaged and stored in state-of-the-art cold storage facilities and dry warehouses. We at Pavi Exim believe that we are the best at what we do, and we strive to live up to our high standards. Customer satisfaction is of the utmost importance. We don’t just do business with our customers, we create relationships of trust.
